LIFE WITH A MUSTACHE

Life definitely changes once you grow a mustache. People see you differently. They expect things from you. Important things. Like knowing how to grill meat properly or fix mysterious household problems.
You start paying attention to grooming in ways you never did before. You learn about oils, balms, waxes, and tiny combs that somehow become essential tools. You discover that wind is both your friend and your enemy.
There are good mustache days and bad mustache days. Some days it sits perfectly. Other days it gets in the way while eating. But you learn patience. You learn discipline. You learn to carry napkins everywhere.
In the end, living with a mustache is more than maintenance. It's a lifestyle choice. A fuzzy, stylish, confidence-boosting lifestyle and honestly, I wouldn't trade it for anything.
